We're putting the finishing touches on our full privacy policy. This page is a plain-English summary of how we approach your data in the meantime — the complete policy will live here soon. Questions? Email hey@mahjongalley.com.

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information like your email address and display name so you can play with friends. Gameplay data (like match results) is stored so your games work across devices.

How we use it

We use your information to run the game — playing with friends, keeping score, and improving the app. We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how the app is used so we can make it better.

What we don't do

We don't sell your personal information, and we don't show third-party ads.

Your choices

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account and its data at any time by emailing hey@mahjongalley.com.

Contact

For any privacy question, reach us at hey@mahjongalley.com.
